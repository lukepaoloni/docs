---
title: "Export Guide"
description: "How to download your shipping rate table as a CSV file."
---

This guide walks you through downloading your shipping rate table from MatrixRates. By the end, you'll have a CSV file on your computer that you can open in any spreadsheet app or keep as a backup.

<Steps>
  <step>
    ## Open MatrixRates from your Shopify admin

    From your Shopify admin, go to **Apps** and click **MatrixRates Import/Export**. The app opens in a new window.

    <Frame caption="The MatrixRates app dashboard showing your current rate table">
      ![MatrixRates app dashboard](/images/export-step-1.png)
    </Frame>
  </step>

  <step>
    ## Click the Export button

    Look for the **Export** button in the app toolbar. It's usually near the top of the page, next to or below the Import button.

    Your browser will instantly download a file called `rates.csv` to your computer.

    <Note>
      The file saves to your Downloads folder. If you're using Chrome or Edge, you'll see the file appear at the bottom of the browser window.
    </Note>
  </step>

  <step>
    ## Open the downloaded file

    Go to your **Downloads** folder and double-click `rates.csv`. It opens in your default spreadsheet app — like Microsoft Excel, Google Sheets, or Numbers.

    You now have a complete copy of your shipping rates that you can view, edit, or share.
  </step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If the file doesn't download automatically, check your browser's download settings or try clicking the Export button again. Some browsers block automatic downloads until you allow them.
</Tip>

Your export is complete. You now have a CSV file with all your shipping rates that you can work with in any spreadsheet app.

<CardGroup>
  <Card title="Edit and re-import" icon="edit-3">
