<Update label="April 2026" description="v1.1.0">
  ### Enhanced Spreadsheet Support
  We've updated our import logic and documentation to support more flexible shipping rate configurations:

  - **Renamed to Spreadsheet Reference** — Our CSV guide is now the "Spreadsheet Reference" to better align with your workflow in Excel and Google Sheets.
  - **New Weight & Currency Units** — You can now specify units directly in your spreadsheet (e.g., `10kg`, `500g`) and use currency codes (e.g., `100 USD`) to override store defaults.
  - **Postcode & City Filters** — Added support for advanced location filtering using wildcards (e.g., `902*`) and comma-separated lists for merchants on Growth and Scale plans.
  - **Automatic Grouping** — Rates sharing the same zone name and location are now automatically grouped together during import.
</Update>
