Importing a rate table is the fastest way to set up or update your shipping rates. Instead of entering rates one by one in Shopify, you can upload hundreds of rates at once using a spreadsheet file.

## Before You Import

<Warning>
**Important:** Importing a file will **overwrite** all existing rates in the selected shipping zone. Always [export a backup](/export-guide) of your current rates before importing a new file.
</Warning>

Before you start, make sure your file is formatted correctly. MatrixRates is very strict about column names and values.

- Review the [Spreadsheet Reference](/spreadsheet-reference) for required columns and data types.
- Ensure your file is saved as a **.csv** (Comma Separated Values) file.
- Check that your Zone names match the zones you have created in the MatrixRates app.

## The Import Process

Follow these steps to upload your rate table to Shopify.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open MatrixRates">
    Log in to your Shopify Admin and navigate to **Apps** > **MatrixRates Import/Export**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Import">
    Click on the **Import** tab in the top navigation menu of the app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your file">
    Click the **Choose File** button and select the spreadsheet file from your computer.

    ![Import screen showing the file picker](/images/import-step-3.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload and Import">
    Click the **Upload and Import** button. The app will begin processing your file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for Confirmation">
    Once the import is complete, you will see a success message showing how many rates were imported. If there are errors, they will be listed row-by-row.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Verifying the Import

After a successful import, it's good practice to verify that your rates are correctly loaded into Shopify.

1. Go to **Settings** > **Shipping and delivery** in your Shopify Admin.
2. Find the **General shipping rates** section and click **Manage**.
3. Scroll down to your shipping zones. You should see the rates you just imported listed under the corresponding zones.
4. (Optional) Use the **Calculate shipping** tool in the Shopify Admin to test specific addresses and order values.
